Pink Mimosa

Description:

Delicious drink for brunch.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups orange juice
  • ½ cup pink sparkling wine
  • 2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers peach schnapps

Steps:

  1. Fill a small pitcher with ice. Pour orange juice, sparkling wine, and peach schnapps over ice; stir well.
